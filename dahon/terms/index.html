Dahon is a personal plant-care companion. You may use it for any non-commercial purpose. The plants, photos, and notes you keep in Dahon are yours.

What Dahon is

Dahon helps you remember to care for your plants. Plant care advice surfaced in the app — watering frequency, light requirements, pet-safety notes — is general guidance compiled from public horticultural sources. It is not a substitute for advice from a horticulturist, vet, or doctor.

No warranty

Dahon is provided "as is." We can't guarantee that reminders will always fire on time (your operating system may delay notifications when battery-saver modes are active), that data will never be lost (back up your device), or that every plant fact in the catalog is accurate for every climate or cultivar.

Pet safety

Dahon flags plants known to be toxic to common pets, but the toxicity database is not exhaustive. If your pet eats a plant, contact a veterinarian — do not rely on Dahon to make that call.
