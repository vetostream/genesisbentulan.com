Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 May 2026

The short version

Dahon does not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. Your plants, photos, care logs, badges, and reminders all stay on your device.

What Dahon stores on your device

  • Plant data: names, species, locations, care logs, streaks, and health notes you enter — saved locally with your operating system's app storage.
  • Photos: images you take or pick to attach to a plant — saved locally inside the app's sandboxed storage.
  • Notification schedules: the times you've asked Dahon to remind you — scheduled with your device's local notification system.

Permissions Dahon asks for

  • Camera: only when you tap "Take Photo" while logging plant care.
  • Photo library: only when you tap "Choose Photo" to attach an existing image.
  • Notifications: for the daily care reminders you set inside the app.

You can revoke any of these permissions in your device settings. Dahon will continue to work with the remaining features.

What Dahon does not do

  • No accounts, no sign-in, no email collection.
  • No analytics, telemetry, crash reporting, or advertising SDKs.
  • No network calls. Dahon does not contact any server about you or your plants.
  • No third-party data sharing.

Children

Dahon is rated for general audiences (13+). It does not knowingly collect data from children, because it does not collect data from anyone.

Deleting your data

You can delete individual plants and photos from inside the app. To erase everything Dahon has stored on your device, uninstall the app — your operating system will remove the app's local storage along with it.

Changes to this policy

If we ever change how Dahon handles data, we'll update this page and bump the "Last updated" date above. Material changes will also be noted in the app's release notes.
